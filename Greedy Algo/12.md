Minimum Candy Distribution
Given n children with ratings, distribute candies such that:

Each child gets at least one candy.
Higher-rated children get more candies than their neighbors.
Return the minimum candies required.

Examples:

Input: ratings = [1,0,2]  
Output: 5  
Explanation: [2,1,2]

Input: ratings = [1,2,2]  
Output: 4  
Explanation: [1,2,1]
